The Three-Body Problem is a science-fiction novel by the Chinese author Liu Cixin. The storyline spans over various time frames and follows humanity's contact with an advanced alien race. The major themes of the book include physics, the search for truth, the impact of technology and science on human society. The book's historical context is China's Cultural Revolution, where the country's scientific and cultural growth was stunted due to political oppression. The book has received glowing reviews and has won multiple literary awards, including the Hugo Award for Best Novel in 2015. The book has also been made into a television series of the same name in 2023, a testament to its success.
